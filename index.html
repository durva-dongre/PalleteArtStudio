Welcome to Palette

A Space Built for Young Artists

A cosy, home-based studio in Panvel where kids aged 6–13 freely express themselves. We balance playful creativity with real, lasting skill-building.

  • Small, friendly batches so every child gets personal attention from Durva directly.
  • Specialising in poster art, acrylic painting, and canvas fabric work — techniques kids rarely learn in school.
  • Warm home-studio setting — relaxed, safe, and welcoming for every child.
  • A space where there are no mistakes — only happy accidents and growth!

Durva Dongre

Founder & Art Educator

A-Grade Exams MAH AAC CET 15 Yrs Practice

A self-taught artist with 15 years of practice, A-grade passes in both Intermediate & Elementary state art exams, and a MAH AAC CET qualification. Durva specialises in poster art, acrylics, and canvas fabric work — and brings all of that passion straight into the classroom!
